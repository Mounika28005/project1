This is an H1 Heading

The <h1> heading is the largest and typically represents the main title of the page.

Content best suited for an <h1> heading includes the primary topic or theme of your webpage, such as the name of your site or the main subject of an article.

This is an H2 Heading

The <h2> heading is slightly smaller than <h1> and is used for major section titles.

Use an <h2> heading for important sections within your content, such as major themes or key categories that support the overall topic.

This is an H3 Heading

The <h3> heading is smaller than <h2>
